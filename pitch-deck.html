How It Works

Live in one afternoon. No IT project.

01

Set up your line

Add your production line, stations, and target cycle times. Takes 5 minutes. No installation.

02

Print QR labels

Create a batch of parts. QR labels print on any office printer. Stick them on the physical parts.

03

Workers scan

At each station, workers scan the QR code with their phone browser. No login. No app download. One tap.

04

AI takes over

Dashboard updates live. AI monitors every 15 minutes. Alerts sent when bottlenecks or failures are detected.
